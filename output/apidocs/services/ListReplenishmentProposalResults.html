ListReplenishmentProposalResults

Lists all Results for a `ReplenishmentProposal`, identified by its `ID`. Through the `PageConfig`'s `Filter` parameter, you have the following filter options available: * `SourceIDs` and/or `TargetIDs` as `OrganizationUnit`.`ID`'s * `ProductIDs` as `Product`.`ID`s * `CustomID` as `Product`.`CustomID` * `BrandName` as `Product`.`Brand`.`Name`
