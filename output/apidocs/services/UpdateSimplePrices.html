UpdateSimplePrices

Update the prices in the pricelist specified. This service takes a simplified approach of the pricing tools EVA offers, but that limits the options you have available. The PriceList to update may only have a single active PriceListAdjustment, and that has to be of type MANUAL_INPUT. * The pricelist is identified by the `PriceListID` or the `BackendSystemID` and `BackendID`. * Products can be identified by their (EVA) `ID` or their `BackendID`
