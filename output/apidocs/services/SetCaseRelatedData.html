SetCaseRelatedData

Set case related data. You can provide an OrderID (optional) You can provide an ExternalOrderID (optional) You can provide further details by setting RelatedItems. These can either - contain a OrderLineID (can only be provided if OrderID is also provided. The ProductID of the orderline will copied to the ProductID of the RelatedItem) - contain a ProductID - a SerialNumber (is settable whether you provided a OrderLineID or ProductID)
