StartCloseCashJournal

Initiates the closing of a cash journal. This does not yet mutate anything, but returns all the details you need about a cash journal to be able to close it. This consists of: - The information about the device and station - Which expenses and deposits were made from this device. - What payments were made. - What the `ExpectedClosingAmount` is, which is a summary of the previous journal's closing amount plus all transactions. - Possible pending cash deposits on this device
