CreateShipmentReceipt

Creates a shipment receipt with `Lines` for specified `ShipmentID`. * `CompletesShipment` / `Lines.CompletesShipmentLine` will close the shipment (line) for futher receipts * `Lines.QuantityReceived` is the delta quantity received in this receipt, so not the new total quantity received * `StockLabelID` is the target StockLabel for the received stock - overrides the StockLabel of the purchase order line
