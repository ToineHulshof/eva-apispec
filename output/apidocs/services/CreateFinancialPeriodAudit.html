CreateFinancialPeriodAudit

Creates a new audit collection of all currently open `Financial Period`s for the given `OrganizationUnitID`. The `OrganizationUnitID` act as root selector and will cascade for all 'child' organizations as well. Supply a `To` parameter to only collect `Financial Period`s from before that moment for audit. Supply the `Type` to specify which or what kind of output you want to have. If none is passed the first available one is used. See the `GetFinancialPeriodAuditOutputTypes` service to fetch all of the available types.
