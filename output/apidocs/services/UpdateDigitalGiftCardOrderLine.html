UpdateDigitalGiftCardOrderLine

This service allows updating giftcard lines created with the `AddGiftCardToOrder` service. Additionally, the `DisplayOptions` property need to be supplied; * `From` and `To` are the names of the sender and recipient of this gift, respectively * `Text` is an additional text to be included on the GiftCard and presented to the recipient (optional) * `ImageWidth` and `ImageHeight` describe the image used
