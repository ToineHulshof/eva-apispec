CreateInvoice

Creates a new invoice for the given `OrganizationUnitID` (the one who writes the invoice) and the `SupplierOrganizationUnitID` (the one who receives good/services). - `InvoiceDate` is the date on which the invoice was created. - `InvoiceNumber` is the unique number for the invoice. - `Blobs` can contain PDF's or other media that can for example be a copy of the invoice - `OriginalTotalAmount` is the amount that is expected on the invoice. E.g. this can differ from the amount that is computed based on the invoice lines - `Type` indicates if this is an invoice for a sales or purchase orders
